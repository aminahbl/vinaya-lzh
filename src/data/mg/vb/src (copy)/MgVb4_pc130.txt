Origin Story

俗人婦女亦如是。乃至已聞者當重聞。
With a lay woman it’s also as above. … Those who have already heard it should listen again:


Final Ruling

若比丘尼不病。令俗人婦女揩摩者波夜提。
If a bhikkhunī who isn’t sick has a lay woman massage her, it’s a pācittiya.”


Explanation

俗人婦女者四種姓家女人。揩摩如比丘尼中說。
“A lay woman” means a woman of the four classes. “Massaging” is as explained in the rule about “a bhikkhunī”.

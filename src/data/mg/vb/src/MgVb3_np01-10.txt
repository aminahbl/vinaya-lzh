Summary Verse

三十事初
Beginning of the 30 matters.

十日離衣宿 非時捉金銀
賣買并乞衣 聽乞得取二
辦衣二居士 王臣次第十
Ten days, being away from one’s robes overnight, out-of-season, taking gold and silver,
Trading, asking for a robe, when allowed to ask one may take two,
Preparing a robe, two householders, kings and officials is the tenth.

如比丘中廣說初跋渠竟。
As explained in detail in the bhikkhu rules. The first chapter is finished.

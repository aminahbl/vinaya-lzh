<h2>Origin Story</h2>


<lzh>沙彌尼亦如是。乃至已聞者當重聞。</lzh>

With a sāmaṇerī it’s also as above. … Those who have already heard it should listen again:




<h2>Final Ruling</h2>


<lzh>若比丘尼不病。令沙彌尼揩摩者波夜提。</lzh>

If a bhikkhunī who isn’t sick has a sāmaṇerī massage her, it’s a pācittiya.”




<h2>Explanation</h2>


<lzh>沙彌尼者隨佛出家受十戒使揩摩者如比丘尼中說。</lzh>

“A sāmaṇerī” means someone who has gone forth under the Buddha, and received the 10 precepts. (If a bhikkhunī) has her give a massage, it should be explained as in the rule about “a bhikkhunī”.

<beginchinese>有三因緣非比丘尼。何等三。心決定壞威儀。有實事僧驅出。轉形為男。如比丘中說。<endchinese>

There are three reasons for which someone is no longer a bhikkhunī. Which three? She decides to break proper conduct<beginnote>i.e. puts on lay clothes, see pakiṇṇaka rule 29<endnote>, there are valid reasons for the sangha to expel her, or she turns into a man. To be explained as for the bhikkhus.
